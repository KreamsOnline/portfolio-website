Case Study Title

Problem Statement

M7Education, a non-profit organization committed to providing quality education resources to underserved communities, faced several critical challenges with their existing website that prompted the need for a UX/UI redesign. These challenges included.

Outdated User Experience: The existing website had an outdated and unintuitive user interface that made it difficult for visitors to navigate and find relevant educational resources. This poor user experience hindered M7Education's mission of delivering educational content effectively. Low Engagement and Conversion Rates: The website suffered from low user engagement and conversion rates. Visitors were not sufficiently engaged, resulting in fewer donations, volunteer sign-ups, and resource downloads. This issue directly impacted the organization's ability to expand its outreach and impact.

Solution

Improved User Experience (UX): The redesigned website provides a more intuitive and user-friendly experience. Visitors can easily navigate, find resources, and access information, leading to increased user satisfaction and longer site visits. Increased Engagement: With an enhanced user interface and improved information architecture, visitors are more likely to engage with the website's content. This increased engagement can result in more frequent visits and interactions with M7Education's resources.
